Academic Programs

Challenging curriculum designed to prepare learners for college and beyond.

Our Academic Divisions

We offer comprehensive programs across all grade levels, each designed to meet students at their developmental stage.

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Our middle school program provides a bridge between elementary and high school, focusing on developing critical thinking skills and academic independence.

  • Core subjects: Math, Science, English, Social Studies
  • Exploratory courses: Art, Music, Technology, World Languages
  • Advisory program for social-emotional learning
  • Extracurricular activities and clubs

High School (Grades 8-12)

Our comprehensive high school program prepares learners for college and career success with rigorous academics and diverse electives.

  • College preparatory curriculum with AP and honors courses
  • Four academic tracks: STEM, Humanities, Arts, and General Studies
  • Comprehensive college counseling program
  • Career exploration and internship opportunities

Honors Program

For learners seeking additional academic challenges, our honors program offers accelerated coursework and independent research opportunities.

  • Advanced coursework across all disciplines
  • Mentorship program with faculty advisors
  • Annual research symposium
  • College credit opportunities

STEM Focus Program

Our STEM program immerses students in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics through hands-on learning and real-world applications.

  • State-of-the-art labs and equipment
  • Robotics, coding, and engineering courses
  • Partnerships with local tech companies
  • Annual STEM fair and competitions
