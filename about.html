Our Story

For over three decades, Mpilo Senior Secondary School has been committed to excellence in education.

Our Mission

Mpilo Senior Secondary School is dedicated to nurturing intellectual growth, creativity, and character in a challenging and supportive environment.

We strive to develop responsible global citizens who are prepared to meet the challenges of a rapidly changing world with confidence, compassion, and integrity.

Vision Statement

To be a premier educational institution that inspires lifelong learning, fosters innovation, and cultivates leadership.

Our History

1901 - Founding

Mpilo Senior Secondary School was established with just 120 students and 8 faculty members in a small building.

1992 - Expansion

The school moved to its current 20-acre campus with new science labs and a library.

2001 - Accreditation

Received accreditation from the National Board of Education, recognizing our academic standards.

2010 - STEM Center

Opened our state-of-the-art STEM center with robotics and computer science labs.

Present Day

Now serving over 1,200 students with 100+ faculty members and numerous academic and athletic achievements.
